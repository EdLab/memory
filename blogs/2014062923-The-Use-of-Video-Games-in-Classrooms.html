The Use of Video Games in Classrooms

Youssef Ballo

June 29, 2014

Video games are increasingly being used in classrooms. According to this survey, the majority of teachers use video games at least twice a week. Most students use Mac or PC computers to access digital games in classrooms. The state of technology and the way games are used appears to be progressing quickly. The use of games can have huge advantages for struggling students. Video games also motivate struggling students to succeed in classrooms. Finding the right game for students is one of the main issues. Websites such as BrainPOP allows teachers to find the right games and assignments depending on the subjects.
