Library Website Update, July 2018

jvazquez

August 8, 2018

It was a hotbed of activity on the site this month! Let’s skip straight to the details.


Overview


Total visits: 8797 (69% off campus | 31% on campus) +71% from last month

Returning visitors: 5509 (63%) +125% from last month


On average, users spent 5 mins 48 on the site, completing 5.4 actions per visit.
