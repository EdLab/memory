Christopher Gu

EdLab Alumni

Never be afraid to take on a new challenge. Often times, they can turn out to be the most rewarding experiences. Several years ago, I was given the opportunity to teach a graduate course in Financial Management at the NYU School of Professional Studies, with less than one weeks notice! As a fresh MBA graduate, I was anxious, but I knew I couldnt pass up this incredible chance. Over the course of the semester, not only did I improve as a communicator, an educator, and a mentor, but I learned as much from my students, as they learned from me.

 

It Is this passion for teaching and continual learning that has drawn me to EdLab. As the Director of Administrative Solutions, I oversee the budget and the day-to-day operations for the Gottesman Libraries. At EdLab, learning never stops. Whether leading or participating in a seminar or workshop, we constantly have the opportunity to teach and learn from one another.
