Christina Kral

EdLab Alumni

Christina Kral is an instant community developer, experience engineer, experimental storyteller, grassroots documentatrice and a constant scholar. Christina was an Eyebeam resident with a particular focus on youth participation models. She has designed a series of story-telling experiments which cross the boundaries of truth, fiction and abstraction. She gave workshops at universities and rural communities around the US and Mexico. Together with Adriana Young she's currently working with the Digital Day Camp kids at Eyebeam and setting up a pop-up community intervention/party.
