Dominic Wright

EdLab Staff

Dominic Wright is currently a freshman at Binghamton University. He plans to eventually major in mechanical engineering and in the near future hopes to become an aeronautical engineer. In order to pursue this career path upon completion of his under-graduate degree he hopes to attend grad school and study in-depth where aerospace technology is concerned. Dominic has also been a player for South Bronx United soccer club. For the past three years he played as a winger, his most recent achievement includes being a part of the 2018/19 premier league winning team. He also plays soccer on the high school level where he has been a part of a City winning team and state runner ups. During high school he was also a part of the National Honors Society.
